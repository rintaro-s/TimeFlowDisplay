Turn your wall-mounted tablet into a focused workstation.

Mi Dashboard is an always-on Android tablet dashboard built with Jetpack Compose.
Clock, calendar, memos, timers, daily alarms, AI chat, webhooks, and image widgets can be combined
into one practical control surface.

Edit mode lets you reposition and resize widgets, with layouts persisted through Room and DataStore.
Wallpaper dimming, language, keep-screen-on, and schedule behavior are controlled in settings.

Editable Widget Board

AI (LMStudio) & Webhook

Configure any LMStudio-compatible endpoint for AI chat.
Trigger saved webhook payloads with a single tap.

Scheduled Screen Dim

WorkManager checks your schedule and applies dim behavior automatically.
Useful for night mode and low-light spaces.
